Pastalight

Writer | Director | Short Film

Details

An eccentric Italian father and his son set out to start their own pasta sauce company. But can they cut it in the merciless world of New York gourmet?

w/ Max Martini “13 Hours,” Adrian Pasdar “Carlito’s Way”, Amanda Righetti “Captain America: The First Avenger”

Distributed on Alaska Airlines in-flight Entertainment
